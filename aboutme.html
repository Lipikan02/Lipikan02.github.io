When the time came for high school, I opted for community college, which provided me with a diverse learning environment and valuable experiences beyond the traditional classroom setting.

My interests are eclectic and reflect my curiosity about the world around me. From delving into the intricacies of FinTech tointerests exploring the thrill of FPV Drones, my pursuits are diverse and ever-evolving. Each interest offers its own unique challenges and rewards, keeping me engaged and motivated in different ways.

Through this portfolio website, I aim to showcase my projects, achievements, and the unique blend of skills and experiences that define who I am. Thank you for taking the time to learn about me, and I look forward to connecting with you further on this exciting journey.
