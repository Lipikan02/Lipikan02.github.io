About Me

Greetings and welcome to my portfolio website! My name is Luc Iñaki Palacios, and I'm delighted to share a glimpse into my journey and passions with you.

Currently, I am pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at the University of Colorado Boulder, with an anticipated graduation year of 2026. However, my educational path has been quite unconventional. As a child, I was homeschooled by my mother, an experience that instilled in me a sense of self-discipline and a thirst for knowledge. When the time came for high school, I opted for community college, which provided me with a diverse learning environment and valuable experiences beyond the traditional classroom setting.

My passions revolve around adventure and a deep curiosity about the world. Whether it's navigating the exhilarating world of FPV Drones, exploring the complexities of FinTech, or hitting the slopes on a snowboard, I find myself constantly seeking new challenges and experiences. Whether I'm on the soccer field, strategizing in a game of Magic: The Gathering, or hanging out with my siblings, each pursuit offers its own set of thrills and rewards, keeping me forever engaged and eager for what comes next.

Since I was young, I've been drawn to programming, fascinated by its logic and problem-solving aspects. I've always loved new technologies and envision myself surfing the wave of AI advancements

Through this portfolio website, I aim to showcase my projects, achievements, and the unique blend of skills and experiences that define who I am. Thank you for taking the time to learn about me, and I look forward to connecting with you further on this exciting journey.
